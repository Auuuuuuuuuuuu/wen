About Wen

A designer rooted in the philosophy of mindful creation, Wen transforms raw materials into objects that speak to the soul. With over a decade of experience in contemporary craft, each piece reflects a deep understanding of form, function, and the beauty found in simplicity.

Working from a studio in Brooklyn, Wen combines traditional techniques with modern sensibilities, creating pieces that are both timeless and distinctly contemporary. Every creation tells a story of careful consideration and artistic integrity.
